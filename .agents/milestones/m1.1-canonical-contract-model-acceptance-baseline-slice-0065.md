## M1.2 Starting Boundary

M1.2 starts after the accepted canonical model boundary.

Authorized M1.2 implementation topics:

- intermediate representation shape;
- schema or manifest extraction mechanics;
- generator location and invocation;
- generated artifact headers and freshness manifests;
- deterministic artifact checks;
- generated TypeScript, mock, shell metadata, or fixture pilot order;
- consumer adoption order and rollback for generated artifacts.

Not authorized as M1.2 invention:

- contract identity meaning;
- contract taxonomy categories;
- semantic, shape, serialization, compatibility, version, evolution, deprecation, or consumer ownership rules;
- normalization rules for identifiers, enums, dates, nulls, collections, names, metadata, ordering, evidence, diagnostics, or compatibility fields;
- allowed or forbidden boundary transformations;
- compatibility, deprecation, fixture movement, artifact movement, or rollback governance.

If M1.2 finds a model defect, M1.1 must be reopened through decision governance with evidence naming the defective model rule and affected consumers.
