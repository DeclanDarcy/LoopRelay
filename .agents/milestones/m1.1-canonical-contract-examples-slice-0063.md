## Architecture Impact

This slice adds M1.1 conformance examples for:

- repository contracts;
- workflow contracts;
- decision contracts;
- execution contracts;
- reasoning contracts;
- continuity contracts;
- governance contracts;
- health contracts;
- certification contracts.

Each example identifies representative identity, category, authoritative source, shape owner, serialization owner, producer boundary, version identity, normalization focus, stability classification, compatibility policy, and governance constraint.

The examples instantiate the existing M1.1 model rather than adding a new mechanism. They are intended to prove that current contract families can be classified without a generator inventing architecture rules.
