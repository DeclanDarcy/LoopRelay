# Milestone 7 Continuity Authority Matrix

## Purpose

Pre-implementation authority audit for Milestone 7 continuity and operational-context transparency. This matrix identifies the current owner, persisted evidence, projection, transport, consumers, and transparency gaps for each continuity concept before new projections are added.

## Matrix

| Concept | Owning service | Persisted evidence | Current projection | Transport | Existing consumers | Gap / next action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Operational-context proposal generation | `CommandCenter.Middle.Continuity.OperationalContextGenerationService` composes inputs, decision analysis, diff, and compression. | Proposal JSON/content under `.agents/operational_context/proposals`; input fingerprints on `OperationalContextProposal`. | `OperationalContextProposal` with fingerprints, semantic changes, and compression summary. | `POST /api/repositories/{repositoryId}/operational-context/generate`; workflow preparation can call `IOperationalContextGenerationService`. | Operational-context UI, workflow preparation, proposal store, workflow projection. | Generation currently discards structured decision-analysis details after producing warnings and generated content. ## Implementation Order Confirmed

1. Add proposal-level assimilation transparency owned by continuity generation/analysis.
2. Add taxonomy basis to the same owner.
3. Add assimilation-limit and omitted-item projection before UI work.
4. Add structured consequences and contradictions.
5. Extend evolution and compression models after proposal-level analysis is explicit.
6. Improve `UnderstandingDiffService` identity-aware matching and semantic change types.
7. Normalize diagnostics categories after the underlying structured facts exist.

## Boundary Notes

- React must render these facts only; it must not classify, score, infer eligibility, infer omission, or reconstruct contradiction semantics.
- Reasoning capture can observe continuity transitions, but continuity remains the authority for operational-context assimilation, diff, compression, evolution, and diagnostics.
- Existing warning strings should remain backward-compatible until typed fields are rendered and tested.
