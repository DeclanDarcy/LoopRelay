# Session Economics & Continuity Infrastructure Roadmap

## Objective

Recover the responsibilities originally carried by the Decision Session Registry and Session Router while remaining consistent with the founder's actual intent.

The purpose of this roadmap is not:

```text
Continuity research
Reasoning preservation
Brainstorm experimentation
```

The purpose is:

```text
Support workflow replacement
through continuity economics,
session economics,
and continuity infrastructure.
```

The founder's original Session Router existed to support:

```text
Decision Throughput
Session Economics
Continuity Efficiency
Routing Decisions
Lifecycle Management
```

not as a continuity research mechanism.

This roadmap restores those responsibilities.

---

# Architectural Principles

## Economics Exists To Serve Throughput

The purpose of continuity infrastructure is:

```text
Higher throughput
Lower cost
Lower latency
Higher continuity quality
```

Not:

```text
Architectural elegance
```

---

## Session Economics Is A First-Class Concern

The original architecture explicitly tracked:

```text
Session Age
Token Usage
Session Availability
```

because those properties affect the economics of decision production.

Session economics is a workflow concern.

Not merely an observability concern.

---

## Continuity Is Infrastructure

Continuity exists to support:

```text
Decision Production
Workflow Progression
Context Availability
```

It is not the primary product.

---

## Infrastructure Must Be Observable

Every continuity decision must be explainable.

Every economic decision must be explainable.

---

## Recovery Is Mandatory

Infrastructure must survive:

```text
Restart
Failure
Provider interruption
Workflow interruption
```

without losing continuity state.
